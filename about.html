About H4TE

Built by players. Driven by competition.

Who We Are

H4TE is a competitive esports organization founded by players who wanted more than just a team — a community built on trust, grind, and passion.

Our Mission

We aim to develop talent, dominate tournaments, and create opportunities for players to grow both competitively and personally.

What We Compete In

From main-stage tournaments to underdog brackets and special events, H4TE competes across multiple formats with one goal: win with style.

Join the Community

Whether you’re a player, supporter, or just love esports — H4TE is open to everyone who respects the grind.
